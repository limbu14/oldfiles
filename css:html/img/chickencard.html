Chickens

All domestic chickens are descendend
from the wild red jungle dowl of india.
They were first tamed 5000 years
ago. and there are now more than
200 different breeds.

Perching Birds

more than 70 per cent of all bird species -
over 5000 species altogether - are
Perching birds or passerines, for example
sparrows and starlings, and this song
thrush shown here. They have feet with
three toes pointing fowards and one
backwards, to help them cling to a perch.
Most build small, neat cup-shaped nests
and they sing a sequence of musical notes.
